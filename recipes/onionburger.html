Onion Burger

Description

This burger was first created during the great depression when hamburger meat was scarce of onions were plentiful. The idea is to create a thin smash burger and to pile as many onions onto the burger as you can.

Ingredients

  • One onion, thinly sliced
  • Hamburger
  • Salt
  • American Cheese
  • Hamburger Buns

Steps

  1. Place cast iron or carbon steel skillet over medium-high heat and begin forming hamburger into balls slighly larger than a golf ball
  2. Once the pan is throughly heated, take a hamburger ball and smash it into the pan using a flat spatula, a burger press, or any object with a flat surface like a small plate. Sprinkle burger with salt
  3. Liberally apply sliced onions on top of the burgers so that they are piled high, roughly 1/4 onion per burger
  4. After a couple of minutes, scrap up the burger from the pan and flip so that the onions are now facing downwards. Add a slice of american cheese on your burger, then add the bottom bun and the top bun on it. This will steam the buns while the burger finishes.
  5. When the burger is done, normally another minute or two, and the onions have cooked up and carmelized a bit, scoop up the burger and slide the top bun underneath and pull out the spatula, flip rightside up for a completed burger.
